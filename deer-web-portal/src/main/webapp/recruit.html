Who are we?

EasyStudy is a China-based online education startup that offers the real overseas pre-school and elementary school curricula in North America to our young students in China. By connecting students in China to English tutors located in North America, we hope to provide students innovative E-learning courses and global education experience.

We believe with the help of real time two-way online conversation technology, our students could have access to interactive support from North American teachers to learn the real North American curricula under CCSS. We are now recruiting online English teachers (part-time or full-time) in US and Canada to tutor students in China.

Our Philosophy

  • Every child should have an equal opportunity to reach high-quality education worldwide.
  • Connect students to best education resources by using Internet technology.
  • Focus on student-centered classes and way of teaching.
  • Cross borders to share different education idea and narrow the education gap.

The role you play

As an English Tutor of EasyStudy, you will be providing online Pre-K and elementary school courses of North America like English, arts, math, science etc., to Chinese students. You can work as a freelancer at home to deliver unique and innovative e-Learning lessons using teaching materials and resources provided by EasyStudy, or you can use your own materials.

Key responsibility

  • Deliver 30-minute live video lessons (mostly 1vs1 online tutoring) to students aged from 5 to 12 using a video conferencing tool provided by EasyStudy.
  • Provide constructive feedback to students to help them improve their English proficiency and understanding of online lessons.
  • Using gestures, pictures or other teaching methods to help students successfully complete the learning goals of each lesson.
  • Teaching time normally occurs at the weekend between hours of 4:00 pm to 7:00 pm (PST).
